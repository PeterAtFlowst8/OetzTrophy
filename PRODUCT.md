# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Experienced whitewater kayakers, race participants, spectators, sponsors, and festival visitors planning around the OETZ TROPHY weekend in Oetz, Tyrol. Registration users are paddlers who need to secure a start place, confirm eligibility, and continue to payment quickly.

## Product Purpose

The site presents the OETZ TROPHY and Ötztal Kayak Festival, explains the race weekend, and converts qualified paddlers into paid race registrations. Success means the event feels serious, alpine, and credible while registration feels clear, trustworthy, and fast.

## Brand Personality

Direct, physical, high-stakes. The voice should feel like whitewater: precise, energetic, and grounded in real river experience rather than generic outdoor inspiration.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic event-ticketing templates, soft SaaS registration cards, municipal tourism layouts, and over-polished adventure marketing that hides the technical seriousness of the race.

## Design Principles

- Put the river first: imagery, language, and layout should keep the Ötztaler Ache and the race conditions present.
- Earn trust before payment: eligibility, rules, age, and legal confirmations must be visible and unambiguous.
- Keep momentum: registration should be efficient, with short labels, predictable fields, and clear next steps.
- Respect the risk: safety requirements should read as part of the sport, not fine print.
- Preserve the event identity: use the existing black, off-white, amber, Agdasima, and Inter system unless there is a deliberate reason to depart.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA for contrast, keyboard access, visible focus states, and readable form controls. Do not rely on color alone for required states or errors. Respect reduced-motion preferences where motion is added.
